About Me

I am the Chief Technology Strategist in Oracle Government and Education organization, with two primary areas of focus:

Inbound Role

Sales Enablement and Solution Strategy messaging for GovEd organization, with primary focus on Data Management.

Outbound Role

Building strategic relationships with SLED, Federal C-execs, Thought Leadership and raising awareness of Oracle solutions at Oracle & Industry Conferences.

Prior to rejoining Oracle, I was the co-founder and CTO of Viscosity North America, where my team was Gold Partner for AWS and Oracle OCI, implementing countless Cloud and on-premise solutions, including ExaCS, ExaCC/C@C, and Exadata systems, as well as Full stack Data Platforms.

With more than 25 years of IT experience, I am a seasoned systems architect who has successfully assisted numerous customers in deploying highly available Oracle systems. I specialize in Oracle Storage, high availability, Oracle RAC, and private database cloud solutions.

Professional Recognition

I am an Oracle ACE Director and also a VMware vExpert. Both of these titles are considered to be the highest recognition in their respective areas.

Academic Involvement

I sit on the curriculum board for the following colleges:

  • Collin College - Computer Systems/Web & Mobile Applications Advisory Committee
  • Dallas Colleges (DCCCD) - Cloud Curriculum Advisory Board

My passion lies in helping organizations understand and leverage their data to create powerful insights and predictive models. As I often say, "Data is the new asset, it's the new gold."
